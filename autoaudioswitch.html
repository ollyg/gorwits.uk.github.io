Welcome to Auto Audio Switch

This is the official home for Auto Audio Switch. A menu bar utility to automatically switch audio connections when your location changes. Great for teleworking between different locations.

Features

  • Set up your input and output list (easy drag and drop).
  • Auto Audio Switch will change audio devices to keep the most preferred always selected..
  • Can be paused when you want manual control.
  • Can be configured to start automatically after login.
